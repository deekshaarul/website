WHO ARE WE ?


We are a group of socially responsible individuals whose sole aim is to mitigate the hurdles faced by society. We as college students strive to be a pillar of support for people on their times of need and desperation to the best of our abilities. We are more than just a blood donation outfit and make it a point to interact with the 'forgettables' of the society such as special children, orphans, senior citizens etc. The fact that any significant change calls for mass involvement or a radical change in public thinking is not missed on us and thus a lot of our work involves spreading social awareness through the means of skits,treks, posters, walks and other ever increasing creative methods. YRC is more than any typical organisation having a stark hierarchy.
We're Family.


BLOOD DONATION


Love Real heroes? Wish you could save lives like they do?

Unleash the real hero in you,You have the most precious super power of all: the ability to save a life by donating blood and platelets!
Help share this invaluable gift with someone in need.
Transfusion of blood saves millions of lives all over the world every year.

The volunteers of Youth Red Cross VIT helps the patients and their families at CMC by sending blood donors but for this we need your cooperation.
All you need to do is give 30 minutes of your life, our team would escort you to the hospital and help you throughout the blood and platelet donation process.

You would in turn not only save a life, you would save a family.
If you are willing to donate blood, please register with us, we would contact you whenever there is a need

Blood is meant to circulate. Pass it around.

MAJOR EVENTS

Be A Genie

Youth Red Cross collects wishes of the children in ANBU ILLAM.The students of VIT who are willing to fulfill the wishes fill a form. We go to Anbu Illam for interacting with the children and giving them the gifts they wished for.

Blood Donation Camp

Organized at VIT every year in collaboration with NSS. A team of Doctors & Staff from CMC Hospital, Govt. Hospital, Vellore and Sri Narayani Hospital participate and collect the Blood during the camp.

5 Star

Youth Red Cross with the help of VIT health centre organize a health check up camp for the staff and students. The parameters checked are blood pressure, eye sight, colour blindness, hearing ability and BMI.

R.O.A.R

Youth Red Cross in collaboration with Peta, organize this event in order to create awareness about the protection of animals and to stop the increasing cruelty against the animals

Training Camp

Youth Red Cross in collaboration with Indian Red Cross society organize a Four Day state level Training-cum-Study Camp for YRC programme officers and volunteers at VIT University.

Old Age Home Visits

Youth Red Cross members enthusiastically interact with the respected senior citizens and listen to their experiences and try their level best to fix a smile on their faces.
